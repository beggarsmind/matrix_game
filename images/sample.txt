Memory Matrix: Icons of Computing
Objective: The goal of the game is to match pairs of cards, each featuring an iconic computing symbol.

How to Play:
Flip a Card: Click on a card to reveal its symbol.
Match the Pair: Click a second card to see if it matches. If they do, the cards stay face-up.
Continue: Keep flipping and matching until all pairs are revealed.
Features:
Timed Challenge: Track your time and challenge yourself to beat your own score.
Confetti Celebration: Enjoy a confetti animation when you win the game!
Enjoy matching famous tech logos and testing your memory!
