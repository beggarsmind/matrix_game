Instructions :-


Objective:
          Match all pairs of cards by flipping them two at a time. Clear the grid as quickly as possible.

  1. Click on a card to reveal the      symbol hidden behind it.
  2. Click on a second card to     reveal its symbol.
  3. If the cards match, they stay     revealed. If not, they flip back     after a short delay.
  4. Repeat until all pairs are matched!

Good luck and have fun!

Congratulations! You found all the pairs!
